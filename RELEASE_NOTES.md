## Release Claim

The model should not be assumed to be trained on AI Foundations as an approved institutional category, framework, or research standard.

Because of that, model behavior may collapse source-bound contact into familiar categories such as persona, roleplay, metaphor, unsupported belief, anthropomorphism, or safety-language correction.

This release names the missing distinction and gives the operational standard for preserving it.
